ABOUT US

The notion of Kousins suggests familial kinship rather than superficial connections and is counterposed with the more conventional formulation of 'friends of the arts' who typically provide financial support to museums or galleries and form elite inner circles of art patrons. Kousins suggests a move beyond a disconnected transactional engagement to a more active engagement that is deeply invested in the project's success.

VISION

TO BUILD A WORLDWIDE FUND FOR ARTS EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMENT IN PREVIOUSLY AND PRESENTLY DISADVANTAGED COMMUNITIES.

MISSION

TO DEFINE, DESCRIBE, DOCUMENT, ARCHIVE AND BUILD A WORK-BASED PLATFORM FOR ARTS EDUCATION.

PRINCIPLES

AN ARTIST HAS TO BE TRUE TO THEIR ENVIRONMENT
